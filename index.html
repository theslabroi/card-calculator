How to Estimate Your Card's Grade

Before choosing an expected value, perform a thorough physical assessment. Inspect surfaces under a bright light for hidden scratches, look closely at corners and edges for micro-whitening, and test the centering ratio. Pack-fresh cards typically score a 9; perfect gem mint 10 grades are highly scarce and should be projected conservatively.
